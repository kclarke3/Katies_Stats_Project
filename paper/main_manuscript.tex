\hypertarget{introduction}{%
\section{Introduction}\label{introduction}}

This project aims to answer the following: How do rates of Drug Induced
Deaths DID overtime vary by state? Drug epidemics have consistiently
reflected the broader social context they take place in. Rates of use,
overdoses, and arrests, can help indicate the significance of policies,
culture shifts, and various other movements. In light of the National
Adderall Shortage, Expansion of Fentanyl Networks, as well as the
pandemic, This paper examines the rates of drug overdose at a state
level over the past five years. Utilizing quantitative methods, this
research aims to contribute to deviance and crime studys that rely upon
geospatial analyses to substantiate community and public health issues,
in this case, drug epidemics
